### 5. $y=1+2x\space\{0\le x\le2\}$

- $s=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}$

- $s=\sqrt{(2-0)^2+(5-1)^2}$
- $s=\sqrt{20}$
- $s=2\sqrt{5}$

- $s=\int_{b}^{a}\sqrt{1+[f'(x)]^2}\space dx$

- $s=\int_{0}^{2}\sqrt{1+(2)^2}\space dx$
- $s=\sqrt{5}\space x \Big|_{0}^{2}$
- $s=2\sqrt{5}$

**Answer:**

- $s=2\sqrt{5}$

### 10. $y=4x^{3/2}\space\{1\le x\le9\}$

- $s=\sqrt{10880}$
- $s=8\sqrt{170}$

- $s=\int_{b}^{a}\sqrt{1+[f'(x)]^2}\space dx$

- $s=\int_{1}^{9}\sqrt{1+36x}\space dx$

- $u=1+36x$

- $s=104.33$
